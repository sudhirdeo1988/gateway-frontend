Watch List Screening Denied Party Screening (often referred to as restricted party screening, sanctioned party screening and watch list screening) is a fundamental element of your company's compliance with local laws and regulations. In our increasingly globalized economy and heightened security, it's more important than ever to Know Your Customer (KYC). Without performing screening regularly and documenting it, you could face huge regulatory fines, sanctions, criminal penalties and, at the very least, costly delays in business progress and revenue.
Import Operations When importing goods, customs agencies around the globe hold the importer responsible for filing accurate data. The ability to validate preliminary data with the final shipment is the cornerstone of compliance.OCR Import Operations streamlines the process of customs clearance, allowing businesses to focus on profit and growth and not waste time on process. Import Operations efficiently diminishes administrative burden and comprehensively assists in the management of all import processes.
License Management Managing an authorization, be it a license, agreement, exemption or exception, national or open general license is not easy. It's not just ensuring that the exports not exceed the authorized quantity or value. But a company's executives have to make sure that all provisos and conditions of the export authorization are strictly followed, including filing periodic reports with the licensing authority when required. Mismanagement of authorizations manually processed may lead to amplified labor.
